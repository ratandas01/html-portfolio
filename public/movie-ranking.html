The Best Movies According to Ratan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Martian

A gripping story about an astronaut stranded on Mars, using science and wit to survive. An inspiring tale of resilience, problem-solving, and human spirit.

Avatar

A visually stunning sci-fi epic set on the alien world of Pandora. A story about courage, connection, and protecting nature against greed.

3 Idiots

A touching story about friendship, passion, and the true meaning of success. It reminds us to follow our dreams rather than just chasing grades.
